My path into CAD comes through design — I approach 3D work the same way I approach graphic design: with intentionality, attention to detail, and an eye for how things will be experienced in the real world.

Working primarily in Fusion 360 and printing on a Bambu Lab P1S, I'm building a foundation in functional and product-oriented 3D design. My graphic design background gives me an edge in presentation, documentation, and thinking through how a design communicates before it's ever fabricated.

This portfolio documents that growth — from early learning projects to increasingly complex functional designs.
